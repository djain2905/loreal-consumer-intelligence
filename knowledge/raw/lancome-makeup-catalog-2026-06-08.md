## Questions?

Makeup can help to elevate your look and enhance your features like your lashes and lips. With makeup products to help you create a natural makeup look or a bold and dramatic one – it’s simple to create a makeup routine with Lancôme.

### What products should I include in my makeup routine?

The products you choose to use in your makeup routine will depend on your desired makeup look and your makeup concerns or environment. For example, if you have issues with your makeup fading and live in a high humidity climate – [using a face primer](https://www.lancome-usa.com/beauty-magazine/makeup/face-primer-for-foundation-how-to.html) is important to help make your makeup last.

### What is a simple makeup routine for beginners?

Tinted Moisturizer/Foundation, Concealer, Blush, Eyebrow Pencil, Mascara, Eyeliner, and Lipstick.

### How do I create a natural look with makeup?

Creating a natural look with makeup comes down to the makeup techniques you use and how much product you apply. Try using a [tinted moisturizer](https://www.lancome-usa.com/makeup/face-makeup/foundation/skin-feels-good-skin-nourishing-foundation/LAN179.html) for a light coverage foundation and only applying concealer where needed. For the eyes stick to a few coats of mascara and fill in eyebrows with a pencil. Swipe on your favorite [lipstick in a nude shade](https://www.lancome-usa.com/beauty-magazine/makeup/the-best-lancome-nude-lipstick-for-every-skin-tone.html) and you’re good to go.

### How do I apply makeup?

Each makeup product will be applied depending on the products formula and type. For [lip makeup](https://www.lancome-usa.com/makeup/lip-makeup/) like lipsticks, lip gloss it’s easy to apply these products using their included applicators. For [face makeup](https://www.lancome-usa.com/makeup/face-makeup/) products like primer, foundation, concealer and cream blush or bronzer — you may like to apply the product using a synthetic makeup brush, your fingertips or a makeup sponge. For products with powder formulas makeup brushes tend to give the best results. Use a face brush to apply face powders, blushes, bronzers, and highlighters and use small brushes and blending brushes to create your [eyeshadow looks](https://www.lancome-usa.com/beauty-magazine-makeup.html).

### How do I make my makeup last?

Making your makeup last (especially in a hot or humid climate, or if you have an oily skin type) can be a difficult task. The key is to adequately [prime your skin with a makeup primer](https://www.lancome-usa.com/beauty-magazine/makeup/face-primer-for-foundation-how-to.html) and look for long-wearing formulas. Our [Teint Idole Ultra Wear Foundation](https://www.lancome-usa.com/makeup/face/foundation/classic-teint-idole-ultra-wear/1000554.html) and [All Over Concealer](https://www.lancome-usa.com/makeup/face-makeup/concealers/teint-idole-ultra-wear-all-over-full-coverage-concealer/00422-LAC.html) are humidity proof and provide up to 24 hours of wear time. For eye makeup look to [waterproof and long-lasting formulas](https://www.lancome-usa.com/beauty-magazine/makeup/waterproof-eye-makeup-essentials.html).

### How can I try on makeup at home?

With our [virtual try on tool](https://www.lancome-usa.com/virtual-makeup-try-on-online.html), [foundation finder tool](https://www.lancome-usa.com/e-shade-finder.html), and our [mascara finder tool](https://www.lancome-usa.com/mascara-finder-us) you can easily try on different shades of makeup at home and find the best products for you without having to leave your home.
